The Art and Science of Snooker: A Detailed Exploration Introduction Snooker, a cue sport that combines precision, strategy, and mental acuity, has captivated audiences worldwide for over a century. Originating in the latter half of the 19th century, snooker has evolved from a leisurely pastime to a highly competitive and professional sport. This essay delves into the history, rules, skills required, and cultural significance of snooker, illustrating why it holds a unique place in the world of sports. Historical Background Snooker traces its origins to India, where British Army officers modified the existing billiards games in the 1870s.
